### 1. Getting Started
- **Login**: Use your student email and password provided by the administration.
- **Dashboard**: View your daily schedule, notifications, and wallet balance.

### 2. Course Enrollment
1. Navigate to "Academic" -> "Course Selection".
2. Browse available sections matching your department.
3. Click "Enroll". You will receive a notification confirmation.

### 3. Attendance
- When in class, the instructor will click "Start Session".
- A QR Code will appear on the projector.
- Open the mobile app -> "QR Attendance" and scan the code.
- **Note**: You must be physically present in the classroom (GPS check).

### 4. Grades
- Go to "My Grades" to view Midterm, Final, and Letter grades as soon as they are published.

### 1. Managing Classes
- **View Roster**: Go to "My Sections" -> Select Section -> "Student List".
- **Email Class**: Use the "Send Notification" button to broadcast a message to all enrolled students.

### 2. Taking Attendance
1. In the classroom, go to "Attendance".
2. Select the current section and click "Create Session".
3. Display the QR Code on the screen.
4. Watch real-time check-ins on the dashboard.

### 3. Grading
1. Go to "Grading" module.
2. Select a student and enter Midterm/Final grades.
3. The system automatically calculates the Letter Grade based on the rubric.
